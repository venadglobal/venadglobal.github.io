🇨🇦 CANADA AIP
5
What is Canada AIP and do I need high CRS points?
▼
The Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP) is a permanent residence pathway for skilled workers in Atlantic Canada — Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, Newfoundland & Labrador, and Prince Edward Island.

No CRS points competition. AIP is employer-driven — you need a job offer from a designated employer, IELTS General Training Band 6+, and a WES credential assessment. Your CRS score does not matter.

Our founder personally navigated this process — no consultant, no shortcuts.

AIP-ക്ക് CRS points competition ഇല്ല. Employer job offer + IELTS GT Band 6 + WES assessment മതി.

🏥 NURSING & ALLIED HEALTH
7
I am a Radiographer — do I register with NMC for UK?
▼
No — this is a critical distinction. The NMC (Nursing and Midwifery Council) is only for Registered Nurses and Midwives.

Radiographers register with the HCPC — Health and Care Professions Council. HCPC also covers Physiotherapists, Occupational Therapists, Paramedics, Biomedical Scientists, and other Allied Health Professionals.

Important: HCPC does not accept applications submitted by third party agencies. You must apply directly yourself. Venad Global guides and prepares you — you submit to HCPC directly. This is the only correct and legal way.

Radiographers register with HCPC — NMC അല്ല. HCPC applications directly submit ചെയ്യണം — third party agencies accept ചെയ്യില്ല. ഞങ്ങൾ guide ചെയ്യുന്നു, നിങ്ങൾ submit ചെയ്യുന്നു.
8
Can GNM holders apply for UK or Canada nursing?
▼
Yes — but the pathway differs from BSc Nursing.

UK: GNM holders can apply for NMC registration as a Registered Nurse — NMC assesses each application individually.

Canada: GNM generally qualifies for Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN/RPN) in most provinces, not Registered Nurse (RN). Some provinces may differ.

Always clarify your specific qualification with us in your free consultation before starting any process.

GNM holders apply ചെയ്യാം — പക്ഷേ pathway BSc-ൽ നിന്ന് വ്യത്യസ്തമാണ്. Free consultation-ൽ clarify ചെയ്യൂ.
