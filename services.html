Frequently Asked Questions

Will you guarantee that I get a visa or job?
No — and anyone who does is lying to you. Visa approvals are decided by government authorities, not consultants. Job offers are made by employers. We provide honest guidance, professional documents, and real support — but we cannot and will not make false promises.
Can I pay in instalments?
Yes, absolutely. A small advance deposit confirms your booking and work begins. The remaining balance is paid in agreed instalments as work progresses. We accept Google Pay, PhonePe, UPI, and Bank Transfer.
What if my application is rejected — do I get a refund?
No refund for visa or job rejections — because we provide guidance services, not outcome guarantees. However, if we fail to deliver agreed documents or services without valid reason, we will discuss a fair refund. This is explained in our Service Agreement.
Do I need a driver's licence or vehicle for Canada?
Not for all roles, but it helps for many positions — especially live-in building manager or operations roles. An Indian driving licence is valid for initial use, and you can convert to a Nova Scotia licence easily once you arrive.
I am a nurse with a GNM diploma — can I go to Canada or UK?
Yes — but the pathway differs. In Canada, GNM typically qualifies for Licensed Practical Nurse (LPN) registration rather than RN. In the UK, GNM qualifies as RGN through NMC. Book a free consultation and we will assess your specific situation honestly.
What kind of websites do you build?
We build professional business websites — brochure sites, service company websites, personal portfolio sites, and bilingual English + Arabic websites. We use proven free and paid hosting platforms. See our own live website at idivet.github.io/cv as an example of quality and style.
